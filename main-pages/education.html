We want to help you develop creative skills through experiential learning.

That’s why we deliver workshops, mentorship, and hands-on learning experiences to young people who want to learn video, design, and marketing.

{% include circle.html color="dorange" size="25" top="20" left="10" z="0"%} {% include circle.html color="black" size="10" top="80" left="80" z="0"%}

1 / 3

We teach workshops.


Our creative team leads weekly workshops on storytelling, videography, design, and more. Check out the workshops that we’ve got coming up in the next month.

2 / 3

We provide mentorship.


We’ll connect you to one of our creatives so that you can shadow them while they work on an ongoing campaign. They can answer your questions and give you a little guidance on where to go next.

3 / 3

You get hands-on experience.


After a couple workshops and some shadowing — we'll connect you, and a couple of other learners, to an organization that needs your support. At that point, what you create is up to you (but we'll always be around to help).
